A — Persistent Side Panel (Right)

Sessions panel always visible on the right. Stays open alongside chat. Future Extensions panel can tab in the same space. Requires terminal width ~120+ cols to feel good.

B — Persistent Side Panel (Left)

Sessions panel on the left, similar to VS Code's file explorer. Chat area to the right. Matches the mental model of "navigation on left, content on right."

C — Collapsible Top Drawer

Side panel lives above the chat as a slide-down drawer. Tab bar shows Sessions / Extensions. Toggle with F2. Full width when open, zero width when closed. Works on any terminal size.

Take a look and click your preferred layout — or let me know in the terminal if you want to mix approaches.
